>> Contact

If you have a problem or simply want to comment on Wicked Shell, there are a couple of possibilities.

  • You might want to address problems to the Wicked Shell mailing list to let others take part in solving that problem. To do so register here for the Wicked Shell mailing list and then write to wickedshell-user [at] lists [dot] sourceforge [dot] net.
  • For direct questions, just write a mail to stefan [at] wickedshell [dot] net.
